Tonic Practice App


Published on March 5, 2025


I discovered an instruments practice app yesterday. It's called Tonic. You can open a room and practice your instrument, and everyone can listen to you. You can also join others to listen them practicing. The app is free, you may earn XP if you practice more, and receive achievements. The design is nice and it gives you motivation to practice.
